Dissolve in Wind

Exploring the emotional and physical impact of Hsinchu's wind

About the Project

Project is an interactive art piece inspired by the unique and relentless wind of Hsinchu. The project uses live camera input to capture human motion and generate dynamic visuals in real-time, projected onto a screen to simulate the effects of wind on form and emotion.

Features

  • Real-time motion capture using a camera
  • Dynamic visual generation to mimic wind distortion
  • Immersive projection to enhance audience interaction
